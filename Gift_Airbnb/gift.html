Gift cards bought on Airbnb.com in the US can be purchased as eGift cards, which are sent via text or email, or physical gift cards that will be shipped to a recipient’s US address.

Airbnb gift cards are available in many countries around the world. You can find the full list of available countries and applicable Gift Card Terms here.

Gift cards purchased in the US can only be redeemed by users who reside in the US. The gift card recipient must also have a valid payment method in the US.

Once you redeem your card and add the funds from the card to your account, you can go to Payment methods in your Account and check your balance.

We currently accept major credit cards and Apple Pay for gift cards purchased on Airbnb.
